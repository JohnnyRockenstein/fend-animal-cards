Tapeti Rabbit

The tapeti also known as the Brazilian cottontail or forest cottontail, is a cottontail rabbit species.

  • Scientific Name: Sylvilagus brasiliensis
  • Average Length: 13 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: Tropical rain forests

Rabbits build nests built of dry grasses above the ground to rear their young. They have a central chamber and three or four smaller chambers at the end of a corridor. The gestation period varies with the geographical location.
